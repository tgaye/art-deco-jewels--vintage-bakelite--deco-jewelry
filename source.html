The Art Deco Era

The Art Deco period (1920-1940) was a time of dramatic social and cultural change, reflected in the bold geometric designs and luxurious materials of its jewelry.

Bakelite, the first synthetic plastic, became a revolutionary material for jewelry makers, allowing for vibrant colors and intricate carvings that captured the modernist spirit of the age.

Our collection focuses on rare and authentic pieces that exemplify the craftsmanship and design innovation of this influential era. Each piece tells a story of the roaring twenties, the machine age aesthetic, and the glamour of early Hollywood.
